Pwntools Tutorials Badge

Lucas Huang
05-28-2026 (Updated: 05-28-2026)
Pwn.College › Badges
badges, learning, pwncollege, thoughts

Why

I originally wanted to work through the pwntools tutorials badge just because I didn’t really know a whole lot about pwntools scripting. I’m still working my way through white belt material, so I just thought that learning pwntools would be pretty useful.

Thoughts

It is a really nice couple of challenges that taught a lot of the basics. I wasn’t expecting anything too hard since I was already semi-comfortable with the very very basics of using the pwntools library. It was really just a way for me to refresh my memory on it.

I also didn’t use AI through it all because what’s the point. I do admit that sometimes, I use AI to answer basic questions during other CTF competitions, but my whole approach to learning cybersecurity is to just minimize the AI use when I’m still learning hte fundementals.
