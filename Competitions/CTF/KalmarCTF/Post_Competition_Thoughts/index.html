Hmmmmmmm….

Ok so I’m actually writing this a couple of months after the competition and all I can say is that I didn’t walk into the competition very confident and I finished learning stuff.

I knew that KalmarCTF was going to be extremely hard and this is the competition I decided to do right after PicoCTF(which is a high school competition). I pretty much only joined to experience something extremely hard. Safe to say I absolutely experienced that.

I was also traveling abroad and didn’t have a lot of time to focus on the competition, so that’s another lousy excuse for you.

Point is, I learned some things about CPU flags and some specific registers that I didn’t previously know. Also, seeing the AstralOS also made me realize that hobby operating systems were possible. Nothing happened in this one, but I’ll still keep it around in this blog just to reminisce at and maybe eventually I’ll be good enough to actually get a placement in this CTF competition.
